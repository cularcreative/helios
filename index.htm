Partnership

Helios can provide solutions to the green hydrogen deficit 

Helios has built up strong partnerships with key players within the financial and scientific communities. We are pleased to announce our strategic partnership with Ascent Hydrogen Fund.

“Ascent’s global mandate is to look for champions across the hydrogen value chain from hydrogen production, hydrogen logistics to hydrogen applications. Following our investment into Hyzon Motors, a champion in the hydrogen mobility sector, we are excited to be now working with Helios, one of the very few companies around the world who have the real technical understanding and execution ability to develop gold hydrogen.” – DW

Ascent Hydrogen Fund is an energy transition investment platform. Run by energy and investment executives. Ascent recently lead the Series A funding round for Hyzon Motors which brought in Total. A global leader in the hydrogen mobility sector, Hyzon recently went public via a SPAC (Decarbonization Plus Acquisition Corp) merger.

Alongside Ascent Hydrogen Fund we are also excited to be working closely with the Durham Energy Institute, the University of Oxford and regional players. With these strong partnerships we intend to lead the global charge in obtaining affordable green hydrogen.

Advisory Board

Professor Jon Gluyas

Professor Gluyas is currently the Executive director in the Durham Energy Institute. In 2015 his research team confirmed a major discovery of helium in Tanzania’s East African Rift Valley – this led to an industry boom for natural helium.

Professor Chris Ballentine

Professor Ballentine is currently the Chair of Geochemistry at the University of Oxford. He worked alongside Professor Gluyas in Tanzania and completed groundbreaking work in measuring noble gas isotopes within the sub-surface.

David Wu

David is currently the President of Ascent Management Funds LLC.  An experience capital markets executive he has brought over 10 companies to IPO across global markets. Previously David was the Group Deputy CFO and Chairman of Capital markets for Fosun International, China’s largest investment conglomerate managing over US$ 100 billion in global assets.
